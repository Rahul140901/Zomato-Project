# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurant Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Zomato Restaurant Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed in **Power BI** to analyze restaurant performance, customer preferences, pricing, ratings, cuisines, and delivery services across multiple countries.

The project transforms raw restaurant data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI dashboards, enabling stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The restaurant industry generates large volumes of customer and operational data. This dashboard helps analyze key performance metrics to answer important business questions such as:

- Which countries have the highest number of restaurants?
- Which cuisines are most popular among customers?
- What is the average cost for two people?
- How are restaurants distributed across different price ranges?
- What percentage of restaurants provide online delivery?
- What percentage of restaurants offer table booking?
- How do customer ratings vary across restaurants?

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- India has the highest number of listed restaurants.
- North Indian cuisine is the most popular cuisine.
- Most restaurants fall within the affordable price range.
- Only a small percentage of restaurants provide table booking facilities.
- Approximately one-fourth of restaurants offer online delivery services.
- The majority of restaurants have customer ratings between **3 and 4**, indicating generally positive customer satisfaction.
